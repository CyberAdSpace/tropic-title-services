What We Offer

Expert Title Insurance & Seamless Closings

At Tropic Title Services, we specialize in providing comprehensive title insurance and seamless real estate closings across Florida. Our expert team ensures a smooth transaction from start to finish, protecting buyers, sellers, and lenders by verifying clear property titles and managing all closing documentation. With a focus on accuracy, efficiency, and customer satisfaction, we are dedicated to safeguarding your property investments and facilitating stress-free real estate transactions.

01

Title Search & Examination

A comprehensive examination of public records to verify the property's ownership history and identify any potential issues, such as liens, judgments, or other claims, that could affect the property's title. This ensures that the title is clear and transferable.

02

Title Insurance

Protection for both homebuyers and lenders against potential legal disputes or financial losses resulting from defects in the property's title. It covers issues like undisclosed heirs, forgery, or incorrect legal descriptions that could arise after the sale.

03

Escrow Services

The title agency acts as a neutral third party to hold and disburse funds during the real estate transaction. Escrow services ensure that the buyer's funds, the seller's documents, and other transaction details are handled securely until closing.

04

Real Estate Closings

Coordination and facilitation of the real estate closing process. This includes preparing and reviewing the necessary legal documents, ensuring all parties sign properly, and overseeing the transfer of ownership from seller to buyer.

05

Lien & Judgment Resolution

Identifying and resolving any existing liens, unpaid taxes, or judgments against the property that could prevent a clean transfer of ownership. The title agency works with the necessary parties to clear these issues before closing.
