What We Do

Comprehensive Title & Closing Services

From title searches to closing day, we handle every detail so you don't have to.

Title Search & Examination

A comprehensive examination of public records to verify ownership history and identify any potential issues such as liens, judgments, or other claims.

Title Insurance

Protection for homebuyers and lenders against legal disputes or financial losses from title defects, including undisclosed heirs, forgery, or incorrect descriptions.

Escrow Services

We act as a neutral third party to hold and disburse funds during your transaction, ensuring everything is handled securely until closing.

Real Estate Closings

Coordination and facilitation of the entire closing process — preparing documents, ensuring proper signatures, and overseeing ownership transfer.

Lien & Judgment Resolution

Identifying and resolving existing liens, unpaid taxes, or judgments against the property to ensure a clean transfer of ownership.

Instant Estimates

Access title quotes, seller net sheets, loan estimates and more — whether you're on the go or in the office with our free online tools.
